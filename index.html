Everything You Need for the
Perfect Karaoke Night

AI Vocal Separation

Powered by Meta's Demucs, automatically strips vocals from any track with full 4-stem separation—vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments.

Multi-Device Magic

TV displays lyrics, phones act as wireless remotes. Connect instantly via QR code with real-time WebSocket sync across all devices.

Word-Level Synced Lyrics

Every word lights up in perfect time. Smooth scrolling, multiple display modes, and auto-fetched lyrics from community databases.

4-Stem Audio Mixer

Independent control over vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments. 6 preset mixer configurations from full karaoke to practice mode.

Battle Mode & Scoring

Participation-based scoring with combo multipliers. Challenge friends to head-to-head battles with persistent leaderboard and hall of fame.

9 Themes + Dynamic Artwork

From Neon Night to Vaporwave—each theme has custom visualizers, particle effects, and glows. Dynamic mode extracts colors from album art.

From Zero to Karaoke in
3 Minutes

1

Clone & Start

Three commands. Docker pulls everything you need—Python, Demucs AI, and the web app.

2

Upload Your Music

Drag and drop MP3, WAV, FLAC, or M4A files. AI processes them in 30–60 seconds.

3

Connect Devices

Open the TV display on your big screen. Scan the QR code with your phone to join as a remote.

4

Sing!

Pick a song, queue it up, and take the stage. Compete for high scores and react with emoji bursts.
