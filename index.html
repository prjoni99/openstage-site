Everything You Need for the
Perfect Karaoke Night

Vocals Gone in Seconds

Drop any song in and AI strips the vocals out automatically. You get a clean instrumental plus separate drums, bass, and melody tracks to mix however you want.

Phones Become Remotes

Your TV shows the lyrics. Everyone's phone becomes a wireless remote—scan a QR code to join, browse songs, queue tracks, and control playback. No app to install.

Lyrics That Follow Along

Each word lights up right when you need to sing it. Lyrics are fetched automatically and scroll smoothly on the big screen so you never lose your place.

Mix It Your Way

Want to keep a little vocal guide? Boost the bass? Mute the drums? Slide individual tracks up and down in real time, or pick from presets like Karaoke, Practice, or A Cappella.

Compete & Show Off

Earn points as you sing, build combos, and climb the leaderboard. Challenge a friend to a head-to-head battle and see who takes the crown. Hall of fame tracks the all-time greats.

Set the Mood

Nine visual themes—from neon glow to concert spotlights to vaporwave. Each one transforms the whole room with matching colors, animations, and visualizers that react to the music.

Up and Running in
3 Minutes

1

Install

One command to start. Everything downloads automatically—no manual setup required.

2

Add Your Music

Drag and drop your songs. The AI strips out the vocals in under a minute and your track is ready.

3

Connect the Room

Put the lyrics up on your TV. Everyone scans a QR code with their phone to join—no app needed.

4

Hit the Stage

Queue songs from any phone, take turns singing, send emoji reactions, and battle for the high score.
